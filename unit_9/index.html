Kulolo



Kuolo is a yellow Labrador. He was born in Pennsylvania on November 15th, 2013.

The Labrador Retriever was bred to be both a friendly companion and a useful working dog breed. These days the Lab works as a retriever for hunters, assistance dog to the handicapped, show competitor, and search and rescue dog, among other canine jobs.

Kulolo has long legs and handsome face. He is always nice and calm. He enjoys the companion with human and needs a lot of attention from the owner. He is also extremely intelligent and has the ability of reading owner's mind with simple eye contact. He is alwaysthere when you need him.

Sai



Sai is a Belgian Malinois. He was born in Maryland on September 16th, 2015.

Canines of the Belgian Malinois dog breed were originally bred to be herding dogs. Today, they also work as police dogs, protection dogs, and family companions. In the hands of an experienced dog person, they are intense, intelligent and athletic companions.

Sai is a smaller than most of the Belgian Malinois in her breed. However, she is extremely fast and palyful. She is always the star in a dog park, running around with pack of dogs benhind her but not able to catch her. She is always alert to strangers yet most affectionate towards the owener.
